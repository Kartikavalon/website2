Safety-first

Your team's safety is not a checkbox.

We built our safety stack specifically for the compliance requirements companies face in NCR — especially for women employees on late shifts.

  • OTP-verified safe drop

    Driver must confirm employee has exited safely. HR is notified if OTP is not submitted within 5 minutes of scheduled drop.

  • IVR check-in calls

    Automated IVR calls to female employees on night duty rides. Distress triggers immediate alert to your HR contact.

  • Driver verification & tracking

    All drivers on-boarded with police verification, Aadhaar-linked profile, and live GPS tracking shared with the employee.

  • Incident response protocol

    24/7 incident response line. Every complaint is logged, investigated, and resolved with a written report within 24 hours.
