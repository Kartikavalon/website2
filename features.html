GPS-Verified Per-Km Billing

💰 Saves 15–35% vs fixed billing

Every Zenoway invoice is generated directly from GPS telemetry — not an odometer reading, driver claim, or vendor estimate. Our vehicles log position every 10 seconds. At month-end, billing is computed from the GPS log and a full route record is attached to the invoice. Every kilometre billed is a kilometre you can verify yourself in under 30 seconds.

This matters because the most common source of inflated transport bills in NCR is fake or padded kilometre claims. Companies on fixed monthly packages or odometer-based billing have no way to audit. With Zenoway, you click any trip in your dashboard and see the exact GPS route, start time, end time, stoppages, and per-km breakdown — all immutable and permanent.

HOW IT WORKS
GPS ping every 10 seconds · Immutable route log · Invoice auto-generated from GPS data · PDF with route map attached to every bill
WHAT YOU GET
Monthly invoice PDF · Route-level breakdown · Driver-wise report · Cost per employee · Year-on-year comparison export

Women's Night-Safety Stack

✓ POSH & Labour law compliant

Our safety stack was designed specifically for the compliance requirements NCR companies face under the Women's Safety Act, POSH Act, and standard HR risk management practice. Every element below is active by default on every contract — not an add-on, not a premium tier.

✓
OTP-Verified Safe Drop
Driver submits system-generated OTP within 5 min of scheduled drop. Non-submission fires instant alert to HR + ops team simultaneously.
✓
IVR Check-In Calls — 8 PM to 6 AM
Automated IVR call every 30 minutes on all night rides. Employee presses 1 to confirm safety. Non-response within 90 sec triggers multi-channel alert.
✓
Live Route Shared Proactively with HR
Shareable live tracking link pushed to the employee AND designated HR contact before every night ride begins. No login, just a link.
✓
Route Deviation Alert within 60 Seconds
Any deviation >500m from approved route fires a triple alert — employee, HR, ops team — within 60 seconds. Driver simultaneously called.
✓
24/7 Incident Response Line
Staffed around the clock. All incidents logged with GPS evidence, investigated, and resolved with written report within 24 hours. Zero open cases in our history.

All safety events are logged and available as a monthly compliance report formatted for HR audit files.

Hybrid-Native Scheduling

📅 Built for 3-day hybrid rosters

Most fleet platforms were built in an era of full daily attendance and then patched for hybrid. Zenoway was built after COVID specifically for the scheduling reality most NCR offices live with today — variable daily attendance, mixed shift patterns, and frequent last-minute changes.

The daily workflow: employees confirm attendance via WhatsApp by 8 PM. Our system takes those confirmations, combines them with the roster and any leave flags, and rebuilds next day's routes and vehicle assignments overnight — automatically, without manual intervention from your HR team or ours.
