Safety stack

Every protocol your HR team
needs to stay compliant.

We designed this specifically for the compliance obligations NCR companies carry — especially for women employees on late shifts and night duty. These weren't features added later. They were the first things we built.

  • OTP-Verified Safe Drop

    When a female employee is dropped, the driver must submit a system-generated OTP within 5 minutes of the scheduled drop time. If no OTP is submitted, an automated alert fires simultaneously to your designated HR contact and our 24/7 ops team. The driver is called within 2 minutes. The incident is logged regardless of outcome — no exceptions, no override.

  • IVR Check-In Calls on Night Routes

    For all rides between 8 PM and 6 AM, automated IVR calls are placed to the employee at 30-minute intervals. The employee presses 1 to confirm safety. Non-response within 90 seconds triggers a multi-channel alert: SMS to the employee, WhatsApp to HR, and a follow-up call from our ops team. If the employee remains unreachable, we escalate to the emergency contact in your HR system.

  • Proactive Live Route Sharing with HR

    Every employee ride generates a shareable live tracking link that is pushed proactively to the employee AND a designated HR contact before the trip begins — automatically, not on request. The HR manager can view any active trip in real time with no login, no app, and no technical setup. The link expires when the trip ends and the GPS log is archived.

  • Route Deviation Alerts within 60 Seconds

    If any vehicle deviates more than 500 metres from its approved GPS route, our system fires a triple alert — to the employee, to HR, and to our ops team — within 60 seconds of detection. The driver is simultaneously called by our ops line. Every deviation is logged with full GPS evidence including pre- and post-deviation route comparison.

  • Zero Unverified Driver Dispatch — Enforced Automatically

    Our compliance engine checks driver eligibility before every dispatch cycle. Any driver whose licence, fitness certificate, insurance, or commercial permit has expired is automatically blocked from being assigned to any trip. There is no manual override. No driver without a complete, current compliance record has ever been dispatched by Zenoway in our entire operating history.

  • 24/7 Incident Response Line

    A dedicated incident response number is staffed around the clock by trained personnel. All reported incidents are logged immediately with timestamp and GPS context, investigated with telemetry evidence, and responded to with a full written resolution report within 24 hours. We have zero open unresolved cases in our operating history. Zero exceptions.

Immutable GPS Telemetry

Every vehicle carries a hardwired GPS unit reporting position every 10 seconds. Telemetry is written to an immutable append-only log — it cannot be edited after the fact. This makes the GPS record the authoritative source for billing disputes and safety investigations. When a driver claims they drove a shorter route, or a safety event needs investigation, the GPS log is the final word. No ambiguity.

Automated Compliance Engine

The system automatically monitors expiry dates for driver licences, vehicle fitness certificates, commercial permits, and insurance across the entire fleet. Any certificate approaching expiry triggers a renewal reminder. NCR regulatory expertise

We track NCR's rules
so you don't have to.

Delhi-NCR has some of India's most dynamic fleet regulations. Our ops team monitors all of them and adjusts your fleet automatically when restrictions apply.

🌫️

GRAP Restriction Management

When Delhi's GRAP escalates to Level 3 or 4 and certain vehicle classes face restrictions, our ops team monitors AQI levels and CAQM orders in real time — automatically flagging affected vehicles for replacement before the restriction takes effect. No action needed from your team.

🔢

Odd-Even Compliance

When Delhi enforces odd-even vehicle restrictions, our system automatically identifies affected registration plates in your fleet and substitutes compliant vehicles before the next dispatch cycle. Continuity maintained without your HR team needing to track or act on the restriction orders.

⚡

Delhi EV Policy Readiness

Delhi's EV transition roadmap will progressively restrict older vehicles in coming years. Zenoway maintains an EV-capable fleet segment and tracks the policy timeline — so your company can transition on schedule without a service gap, with full ESG reporting data on every EV trip.
