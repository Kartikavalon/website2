Our story

Why Zenoway exists.

Before Zenoway, our founding team spent five years inside some of India's largest companies — not as consultants, but as the people actually running the fleet. We managed employee transport for Mahindra & Mahindra's manufacturing and corporate campuses, overseeing 800+ vehicles across multiple locations and states. We ran route planning, driver compliance, shift coordination, and billing operations for Tata Group entities spanning NCR, Pune, and Bengaluru. And we did the same at other major Indian enterprises — developing logistics systems, optimising supply chain transport, and managing the full operational complexity of large corporate fleets.

We know what fleet management failures look like — not from a case study, but from fixing them at 2 AM. A driver who didn't show because no one tracked his licence expiry. A billing dispute that took three weeks because there was no GPS record. A woman waiting alone at 11 PM because the IVR call wasn't configured correctly. These are real situations we dealt with, and they're exactly what Zenoway's systems were built to prevent.

When we looked at what mid-size NCR companies were being offered — either over-engineered enterprise SaaS platforms that treated them as an afterthought, or local vendors with no technology and no accountability — we saw the same gap we'd experienced firsthand. There was no provider doing the right things for companies between 20 and 500 employees.

So in 2024, we built Zenoway. Not as a pivot or a SaaS experiment — but as the service we would have bought when we were on the other side of the table, managing transport for hundreds of employees and needing a vendor who actually understood what they were doing.

What we believe

The principles we operate on.

These aren't brand values written for a website. They're the lessons we learned from five years of running real fleets — and the non-negotiables we designed Zenoway around.

01

Accountability must be automatic

The biggest failure mode in fleet management is relying on humans to check things manually. Driver licence expired — someone should have caught that. OTP wasn't submitted — someone should have noticed. At Zenoway, accountability is built into the system so humans don't have to be the failsafe.

02

Billing must be verifiable, not just trustworthy

We don't ask clients to trust us on billing. We give them a GPS route log attached to every invoice so they can verify it themselves in 30 seconds. Trust is built by making verification easy — not by asking clients to accept a number on a PDF.

03

Mid-size teams deserve enterprise-grade ops

A 150-person company in Noida has the same safety obligations and compliance requirements as a 15,000-person company. They don't have the same budget — but they deserve the same quality of fleet operations. That's the gap Zenoway fills.

04

The right technology is invisible

A platform that requires IT sign-off, app installations, and staff training has failed at the product level before it's even deployed. The best technology in employee transport is the kind employees never have to think about — it just works, through WhatsApp, without friction.

05

Human response beats AI response every time

When a driver doesn't show at 7 AM, an AI chatbot is not what your HR manager needs. A person on WhatsApp who knows the route and is already working on a solution — that resolves the problem. Our 15-minute SLA is a person, not an automated acknowledgement.

06

NCR is not a generic market

Delhi-NCR has GRAP restrictions, odd-even rules, specific corridor congestion, and a regulatory environment that changes regularly. Running fleet operations on a generic India platform means accepting generic India performance. Zenoway was built for NCR specifically — roads, regulations, and all.
