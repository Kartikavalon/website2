What we offer

Everything your NCR team needs.
Tailored to company's operational requirements.

Zenoway provides dependable corporate transport for small and mid-size offices across Gurugram and Noida. Built around the needs of growing companies, our service focuses on reliability, transparent billing, and strict safety compliance. We combine professional operations with flexible service so your team can travel comfortably and on time, every day. With clear communication, accountable processes, and a commitment to consistent service quality, Zenoway helps businesses manage employee transport with confidence while keeping costs practical and operations smooth.

📍

GPS-Verified Per-Km Billing

Every invoice is generated from actual GPS telemetry — not an odometer reading or driver estimate. A full digital route log is attached to every bill. Every kilometre billed is a kilometre you can verify yourself in 30 seconds. Clients switching from fixed-rate billing save 15–35% on average.

💰 15–35% savings vs fixed billing
🛡️

Women's Night-Safety Stack

OTP-verified drop confirmation, IVR check-in calls every 30 minutes on all night rides, live tracking shared proactively with your HR contact, and instant automated alerts if anything goes wrong. Built to meet POSH and Women's Safety Act requirements out of the box — active by default on every contract.

✓ POSH compliant · Active by default
🔄

Hybrid-Native Daily Scheduling

Built from scratch for 3-day hybrid rosters. Employees confirm attendance via WhatsApp by 8 PM. Routes self-optimise overnight. Variable attendance, ad-hoc cancellations, last-minute additions — all handled automatically without anyone manually replanning.

📅 Handles variable attendance daily
📊

Live Admin Dashboard

Real-time view of every active vehicle, ETA, driver detail, and trip status — in any browser, no installation. Monthly GPS-verified invoices auto-generate. One-click compliance reports and safety logs ready for any HR audit. Your team needs zero technical training to use it.

🌐 Zero IT setup · Works on any device
⚡

EV & GRAP-Compliant Fleet

Request electric vehicles for your ESG reporting. Our operations team tracks Delhi GRAP restriction levels, odd-even rules, and NCR emission norms in real time — automatically rerouting or substituting vehicles when regulations change. No action needed from your team, ever.

🌿 ESG reporting data included
🤝

Dedicated Account Manager

Every Zenoway client gets a named human on WhatsApp, who knows your routes, shift patterns, and HR contact by heart. No call centres, no ticket numbers, no bots. Response SLA: 15 minutes. Escalation: 1 hour, every time, guaranteed.

⏱ 15-min response · Real human
🗺️

Proprietary NCR Route Engine

Our routing model is trained on 5+ years of real NCR fleet data — DLF Cyber City peak hours, Noida Expressway night patterns, NH-48 morning congestion, Dwarka Expressway windows. Routes built on our model outperform Google Maps by 18–22% in actual on-time delivery across our fleet.

🧠 NCR-native · Not generic maps
🔍

Automated Driver Compliance

All drivers are Aadhaar-verified and police-verified before their first trip. Our system automatically tracks licence expiry, fitness certificate renewals, insurance, and commercial permit validity — blocking non-compliant drivers from dispatch with no manual oversight required.

🪪 Zero unverified drivers · Ever
📱

WhatsApp-Native for Employees

Employees confirm attendance, track their driver live, and report issues entirely through WhatsApp. No app download, no new login, no IT helpdesk calls. Adoption in our pilots: 97% — compared to 40–60% for purpose-built transport apps. It works because employees already use it every day.

💬 97% adoption in pilots
