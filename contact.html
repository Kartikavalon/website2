Common questions

Things companies usually
ask us first.

How is Zenoway different from a local transport vendor?

A local vendor gives you vehicles and a monthly bill with no audit trail. Zenoway gives you GPS-verified billing, a live admin dashboard, automated safety protocols, driver compliance monitoring, and a named account manager on WhatsApp. The accountability difference is significant — and so is the 15–35% cost saving from GPS per-km vs fixed-rate estimates.

Do employees need to download an app?

No. Employees interact entirely through WhatsApp — confirming attendance, tracking their driver live, and reporting issues. No app download, no new accounts, no logins. Our pilots showed 97% employee adoption for the WhatsApp workflow vs 40–60% for transport apps. We optimised for real-world adoption, not feature checklists.

How does the billing actually work?

Every vehicle logs GPS position every 10 seconds. At month-end, your invoice is generated from the GPS log — not driver reports or odometer readings. Each line item links to a verifiable GPS route. The log is immutable, so there is no scope for inflated claims. If it wasn't driven, you don't pay for it.

What does the onboarding look like?

You share your employee roster (Excel or WhatsApp). We deliver a route plan and cost breakdown in 24 hours. Once approved, we assign verified drivers, brief employees via WhatsApp, and activate your dashboard. First vehicle on road within 72 hours of sign-off. No IT team, no procurement cycle.

What happens if a driver doesn't show up?

Your account manager is on WhatsApp from 6 AM. If a driver misses a pickup, you message — and within 15 minutes, a replacement is arranged or an alternative is coordinated. We track driver availability before each dispatch cycle, so most no-shows are caught the night before and pre-resolved. A person handles it — not a ticket system.
