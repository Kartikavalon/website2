Base Rate Structure

Every trip has a fixed base charge of ₹CONFIG.BASE_FIXED_COST regardless of distance. On top of this, a per-km variable rate of ₹CONFIG.RATE_PER_KM/km is applied. This ensures short-distance trips are viable for Zenoway to operate.

Peak Hour Pricing

Pickup peak is CONFIG.PEAK_PICKUP_HOUR:00 and drop peak is CONFIG.PEAK_DROP_HOUR:00. The farther your office hours deviate from these peaks, the lower your multiplier (down to CONFIG.MIN_PEAK_MULTIPLIER×). At peak times the multiplier is 1.0×.

Solo vs Shared Travel

Shared cab rides are calculated at standard rate. Solo (exclusive) rides carry a CONFIG.SOLO_MULTIPLIER× multiplier since the vehicle is reserved for a single employee and cannot carry others on the same route.

Monthly Calculation

Cost = (BASE_FIXED + dist × RATE_PER_KM) × solo_mult × peak_mult × 2 trips/day × working_days_per_month × employees. Working days = days/week × 4.33 weeks.

Plan Discounts

Monthly: no discount. Quarterly (3 months): 5% off. Half-Yearly (6 months): 10% off. Yearly (12 months): 15% off. Coupon discounts stack additively on top of plan discounts.

GST

GST at CONFIG.GST_RATE×100% is applied after all discounts on the net monthly amount. The total shown is the final payable amount inclusive of all taxes.
