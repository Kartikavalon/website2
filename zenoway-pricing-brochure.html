PRICING STRUCTURE
Two-part cost model
Every trip has a fixed base charge plus a variable distance-based component.
Cost per trip formula
Trip Cost = (₹80 base + distance × ₹18/km)
           × solo_multiplier (1.0× shared | 1.4× solo)
           × peak_multiplier (0.70× – 1.00×)

Monthly = Trip Cost × 2 trips/day × working_days × employees
📍
Fixed Base ₹80/trip
Minimum viable trip cost. Ensures short-distance pickups remain operationally sustainable.
🛣️
₹18/km Variable
GPS-verified distance. You pay only for actual km driven — no estimates, no inflated odometers.
🚗
Solo Premium 1.4×
Exclusive cab for one employee. Vehicle reserved, no co-passengers — priced accordingly.
PEAK HOUR LOGIC
Off-peak timing = lower rates
Peak demand is 10:00 AM pickup / 6:00 PM drop. The farther your timings deviate, the cheaper your rate — down to a 30% maximum discount.
